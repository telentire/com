What is CNC Machine?

  • John Doe
  • Jan 1, 2020
  • 12 Comments

Today CNC-VMC Machine plays the most important role in the manufacturing process, the full form of cnc means Computer Numerical Control or Computerized Numerical Control. With the help of these machines, any machining can be automated by their program, which increases the speed of manufacturing and also improves the quality of each job. So here we will know some basic information about cnc machine in hindi, types of cnc machine, types of cnc machine in hindi etc.

CNC Machine: CNC Machine which is also called Numerical Control Machine in Hindi. which uses commands through programs to operate automatically without an operator. That is, this CNC machine follows the instructions of a coded program to automatically start without a manual process controlling the machining operation. There are many benefits with the help of cnc vmc machines, such as reduction of defects, better speed, better accuracy, and better productivity levels, etc.

Now a day without cnc machines you can't amazine the revolution in future.

Types of CNC Machine

There ar so many CNC Machines,Lets start to explore on by one.

Turning Center

This type of machine has two axis, one X axis and the other Z axis, the machining of job on this machine is done in circular direction, hence it is also called CNC Turning or CNC Lathes. These machines are mostly used to make those jobs, whose shape is complex and that job cannot be done in manual lathe machine and its production is also more, then this CNC Turning or CNC Lathes machine is used. .

Vertical Machining Center

In VMC Machine the job is fixed on the machine bed and the movement of this machine bed is in the "X" and "Y" axis, and the movement of the cutting spindle is in the vertical position i.e. in the "Z" axis That's why it is called VMC machine.

Horizontal Machining Center

In HMC Machine also the work job is fixed on the machine bed and the movement of the machine bed is in "X" and "Y" axis, and the position of the cutting spindle is in horizontal, i.e. in spindle horizontal orientation. That's why it is called HMC machine. HMC machines also come in 5-axis.

Turno Mill

TM - Turno Mill machines require only one set-up. This means that it produces more output in less time and less handling. And apart from this, instead of two machines, production is done in one machine, that is, it can do both types of work.

CNC Router Machine

CNC Router Machine This is a CNC machine, this machine is used for various types of hard materials, wood, composites, aluminum, steel, plastics, glass and foams. By the way, CNC routers are available in 3-axis and 5-axis. Many manufacturers also offer 5Axis capabilities and the A and B Axis for the rotary 4th axis.

CNC Laser Cutter Machine

This CNC Laser Cutter Machine uses a specialized commercial laser to cut the material, the CNC follows it according to the pattern to cut the material, including all G-codes and M-codes. is used.

In this, a laser beam is directed at the material, melting the material, and it is blown away by a jet of gas, thus cutting the material. Industrial laser cutters are used for flat sheet, piping, profile cutting, and more.
